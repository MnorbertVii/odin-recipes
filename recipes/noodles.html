Ingredients.

  • 1/3 cup red miso paste
  • 3 tablespoons maple syrup
  • 1/4 cup rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 medium yellow onion, sliced
  • 3 clove cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 2/3 cups water

Directions.

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Combine red miso paste, maple syrup, rice vinegar, soy sauce,
    garlic powder, black pepper, kosher salt, and sriracha in a
    mixing bowl and whisk until smooth.
  3. Add beef to the bowl and coat thoroughly on both sides, poking beef all over
    with a fork to allow the marinade to soak into the meat.
  4. Add onions and minced garlic to a braising pan, and distribute evenly.
  5. Top with beef, and all the sauce mixture. Spread sauce over top of beef, and
    add 2/3 cup water around the meat.
  6. Transfer pan to the preheated oven and roast for 1 hour. Remove from oven, flip
    beef over, and spoon pan drippings over the top. Return to the oven and roast for
    1 hour more. Cover pan and continue roasting for 1 more hour.
  7. Serve immediately with cilantro and green onions if desired.
